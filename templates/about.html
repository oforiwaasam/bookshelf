About Us

WE LOVE BOOKS, JUST LIKE YOU!

Our goal is to provide a platform where users are able to enhance their reading habits while getting cheaper options to get books. Users will be able to keep track of their progress on the user page and will be able to get book options when they search for any books, authors, ISBNs, or authors. There will also be suggestions of books from the New York Times Bestsellers Lists as well as suggestions based on other users’ reading habits.


Mission Statement

GET PEOPLE ACCESS TO BOOKS, SO THEY CAN READ, AND DISCOVER THE WORLD!

Whether you're in college or simply enjoy reading, we strive to provide the best access to your favorite books and textbooks. We pull our data from the best resources to help you find the books you need at a very low price. We also keep track, for you, of the books you love and recommend related books.
